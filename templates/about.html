About Us

As you can tell, I love baking, well I really, really, really love baking! It’s a passion I have since childhood. I’ve been baking for over 25 years for kids' school events, the local fire house, and my friends at the hospital where I work as a labor and delivery nurse.

I started Baker Recipes to share some of my favorite baking recipes with you. Whether you are a home novice baker or a professional master baker, my goal is to inspire you with easy-to-make baking recipes from Desserts to Bread and more. So are you ready to start baking? I am! Let’s do it together and if you have any questions along the way just send me a message and I’ll do my best to respond.

This site is open for anyone to contibute and share their favorite recipes!
